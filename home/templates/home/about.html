About

As college students, we know budgeting can be difficult. We have limited income and are short on time due to homework, studying, and extracurriculars. We simply do not have the time to budget, but it is extremely important to do so during these fiscally trying times.

Our Solution: Money Parce - A Web Application which allows users to organize their finance and budget for future in a simple and clear way.

What utilities does Money Parce provide?

  • Allows users to input their income by simply linking their bank so they don't have to waste their time
  • If they'd like, users can also input their spending manually
  • Categorizes their spending and sets budgets for them
  • Provides personalized written tips and visual reports to show the user how they can improve their money management in an easily digestible manner
